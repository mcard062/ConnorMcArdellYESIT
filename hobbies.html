My Passions & Interests

Discover the activities that shape who I am

Boy Scouts

I first started Boy Scouts in Kindergarten as a Lion scout. I mostly did it because I knew a lot of friends in it and I also loved to go camping. Over the years I would grow to love the program even more and go on so many amaizing trips.

My Journey

My favorite adventure in Boy Scouts was the trip to the Florida Sea Base. It was a 9 day long trip but the adventure made it feel like a lifetime. I got to do many cool things from holding aligators to swimming with sharks.

Why It Matters

This matters to me because it taught me how to be a leader. It gave me the confidence to do many things I would not normally do in life.

Orchestra

I started Orchestra in the 6th grade. It was required to play a music class but I ended up loving to play the violin. I went on to play in Orchestra all throughout High School.

Memorable Experiences

My favorite memory was when I was in the Stirling Strings and we were going to play at St. Scholastica and the power went completely out. We decided to play our music from memory in the dark for the audiance and when we played our final song the lights turned on. It was an amazing performance.

Personal Growth

Orchestra helped me get out of my comfort zone. I have been an anxious person for a long time and having to play on stage infront of so many people made me feel amazing even though I was nervous. I also met friends that I still talk to today.
